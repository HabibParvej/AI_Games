About These Games

These interactive puzzles demonstrate classic problems in artificial intelligence, including search algorithms, state space representation, and constraint satisfaction.

Each game presents a unique challenge that requires logical thinking and problem-solving skills to complete. Students can experiment with different strategies to find optimal solutions.

Missionaries & Cannibals

Get all missionaries and cannibals across the river safely

Rules:

  • The boat can carry at most two people
  • Cannibals cannot outnumber missionaries on either side
  • All must cross the river safely
Play Game

Water Jug Challenge

Measure exact amounts using jugs of different capacities

Rules:

  • Fill, empty, or transfer water between jugs
  • No measuring marks - use the jug capacities
  • Reach the target amount with the fewest moves
Play Game

River Crossing

Get all characters across the river with constraints

Rules:

  • Boat can only carry limited passengers
  • Certain characters cannot be left unsupervised
  • Find the optimal sequence of crossings
